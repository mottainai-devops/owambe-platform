## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Accessing the Platform

**URL**: `https://owambe.com` (or your deployed URL)

**Supported Browsers**:
- Google Chrome (recommended)
- Mozilla Firefox
- Safari
- Microsoft Edge

**Device Compatibility**:
- Desktop computers
- Tablets
- Mobile phones (responsive design)

### 1.2 Creating an Account

The Owambe platform uses **Manus OAuth** for secure authentication. You don't need to create a separate password.

**Steps to Sign In**:
1. Click the **"Sign In"** button in the top-right corner
2. You'll be redirected to the Manus authentication page
3. Enter your credentials or create a new Manus account
4. Grant permission for Owambe to access your profile
5. You'll be redirected back to Owambe, now logged in

## 5. Troubleshooting & FAQ

### 5.1 Common Issues

**Issue: Cannot Sign In**
- **Solution**: 
  - Ensure you have a Manus account
  - Check your internet connection
  - Clear browser cache and cookies
  - Try a different browser
  - Contact support if issue persists

**Issue: Booking Not Showing in Dashboard**
- **Solution**:
  - Refresh the page
  - Check if you're logged in
  - Verify booking was completed (check for confirmation message)
  - Wait a few minutes for system to sync
  - Contact support with booking details

**Issue: Cannot Upload Images**
- **Solution**:
  - Check file size (max 5MB per image)
  - Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP
  - Check internet connection
  - Try compressing image
  - Use a different browser

**Issue: Payment Not Processing** (Future)
- **Solution**:
  - Verify payment details
  - Check card balance
  - Try different payment method
  - Contact your bank
  - Contact support

### 5.2 Frequently Asked Questions

**General Questions**:

**Q: Is Owambe free to use?**
A: Yes, creating an account and browsing is free. You only pay when booking services.

**Q: How do I cancel a booking?**
A: Go to Dashboard → Bookings, find your booking, and click "Cancel". Refund policy depends on the cancellation timing.

**Q: Can I modify my booking?**
A: Contact the partner directly through the booking details page to request modifications.

**Q: How do I contact customer support?**
A: Click "Help Center" in the footer or email support@owambe.com

**For Customers**:

**Q: How do I know if a partner is verified?**
A: Look for the blue verification badge on their profile.

**Q: When do I pay for bookings?**
A: Payment terms vary. Some require full payment upfront, others accept deposits.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Refund policies vary by partner. Check the cancellation policy before booking.

**Q: How do I leave a review?**
A: After your event/service is completed, you'll receive a prompt to leave a review.
