# Owambe Platform - User Guide

## Website Overview

**Purpose:** Owambe is a comprehensive event, venue, and hotel booking platform that connects event planners with stunning venues, professional hotels, and top-tier vendors all in one place.

**Access:** Public - Anyone can browse events, venues, and hotels. Login required for booking and advanced features.

**Tech Stack:** Built with React 19, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS 4, Express.js, tRPC 11, MySQL database, and Manus OAuth authentication.

---

## Powered by Manus

This platform is built on cutting-edge technology:

- **Frontend:** React 19 with TypeScript for type-safe components, Tailwind CSS 4 for responsive design, and shadcn/ui for accessible, beautiful components

- **Database:** MySQL with Drizzle ORM for robust data management

## Using Your Website

### 1. **Browsing Events, Venues & Hotels**

Start by exploring what's available:

- Click "Browse Events" on the homepage to see all upcoming events
- Navigate to "Venues" to discover available event spaces
- Check "Hotels" for accommodation options
- Use the advanced search filters to narrow down by category, location, price, and rating

### 2. **Making a Booking**

Once you find what you like:

1. Click on an event, venue, or hotel card to view full details
2. Review the description, amenities, pricing, and customer reviews
3. Select your dates and quantity (guests for events/venues, rooms for hotels)
4. Click "Book Now" to proceed to checkout
5. Complete payment through our secure Paystack integration
6. Receive instant confirmation and booking details

### 3. **Leaving Reviews & Ratings**

Share your experience:

1. After your booking is complete, visit the detail page for that item
2. Scroll to the "Reviews" section at the bottom
3. Click "Write a Review" and rate your experience (1-5 stars)
4. Add a detailed comment about what you loved or could improve
5. Submit your review - it helps other users make informed decisions

### 4. **Managing Your Bookings**

Track all your reservations:

1. Click "My Bookings" in the navigation menu
2. View upcoming and past bookings with full details
3. Cancel bookings if needed (subject to cancellation policy)
4. Download invoices and receipts for your records

### 5. **Earning Loyalty Points**

Accumulate rewards with every booking:

1. Each booking automatically earns loyalty points based on spending
2. View your points balance in your dashboard under "Loyalty Program"
3. Progress through tiers: Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum
4. Higher tiers unlock exclusive discounts and benefits
5. Redeem points for future bookings or special offers

### 6. **Staying Updated with Notifications**

Never miss important updates:

1. Click the notification bell icon in the top navigation
2. View all your notifications (booking confirmations, payment updates, promotions)
3. Click "Mark as Read" to dismiss notifications
4. Customize notification preferences in your account settings

### 7. **Messaging Other Users**

Connect directly with vendors and service providers:

1. Click "Messages" in the navigation to access your conversations
2. Start a new conversation or continue existing chats
3. Send messages to discuss details, ask questions, or negotiate terms
4. All messages are stored for future reference

## Managing Your Website

### Dashboard & Settings

Access your account management through the Management UI:

- **Settings Panel:** Update your profile, change password, manage notification preferences
- **Dashboard:** View booking statistics, loyalty points, and account overview
- **Database:** View your bookings and transaction history (admin access only)
- **Notifications:** Configure email, SMS, and push notification preferences

### Customization

Personalize your experience:

1. Go to Settings → General
2. Update your profile picture and bio
3. Set your preferred notification channels (email, SMS, push)
4. Choose which notification types you want to receive

## Key Features

### Advanced Search & Filtering

Find exactly what you need:

- Filter by category, location, price range, and minimum rating
